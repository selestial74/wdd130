Baguio City, Philippines

Baguio City is known as the "Summer Capital of the Philippines" because of its cool climate and refreshing mountain air. Located in the Cordillera region, it is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the country.

Many Filipinos love Baguio City for its breathtaking landscapes, pine trees, and peaceful atmosphere. The city is also famous for strawberries, which are grown in nearby La Trinidad and sold fresh in markets and along the streets.

With scenic views, foggy mornings, and iconic spots like Burnham Park and Mines View Park, Baguio offers a relaxing escape from the heat and busy lifestyle of lowland cities.
